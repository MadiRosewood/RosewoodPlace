Zelda's Moveset

Standard Attacks

  • Jab: Quick punches with her fists.
  • Forward Tilt (F-Tilt): A quick forward kick.
  • Up Tilt (U-Tilt): An upward kick.
  • Down Tilt (D-Tilt): A low kick.

Smash Attacks

  • Forward Smash (F-Smash): A powerful swipe with her hand.
  • Up Smash (U-Smash): An upward thrust that creates a small explosion.
  • Down Smash (D-Smash): A dual kick attack that hits on both sides.

Aerial Attacks

  • Neutral Air (N-Air): Spins in the air, dealing damage.
  • Forward Air (F-Air): A quick forward kick.
  • Back Air (B-Air): A backward kick.
  • Up Air (U-Air): An upward kick.
  • Down Air (D-Air): A downward thrust with her body.

Special Moves

  • Neutral Special (N-Special) - Din's Fire: Creates a fireball that can be charged and released.
  • Side Special (F-Special) - Nayru's Love: A shield that reflects projectiles and deals damage.
  • Up Special (U-Special) - Farore's Wind: Teleports Zelda a short distance; she can control the teleport's direction.
  • Down Special (D-Special) - Phantom Slash: Summons a phantom that can charge and attack.

Final Smash

  • Triforce of Wisdom: Zelda summons the Triforce, unleashing a powerful attack.

Tips for Playing as Zelda

  • Spacing: Use her projectiles (Din's Fire and Phantom Slash) to control space.
  • Zoning: Keep opponents at bay with her strong ranged attacks.
  • Teleports: Use Farore's Wind strategically for surprise attacks or escapes.
